AJ’s Highway Tuckshop

About Us

Welcome to AJ’s Highway Tuckshop is a small business that provides delicious and affordable snacks and drinks to the travelers and locals of Pella, Northern Cape in South Africa. Established in 2022 by Anthea Julie, a passionate and friendly entrepreneur, AJ’s Highway Tuckshop aims to satisfy the hunger and thirst of its customers with a variety of products.


Our Mission Our mission is to serve our customers with quality and convenience. We do this by offering a selection of tasty and fresh snacks and drinks, at reasonable prices and with fast service. We also use locally sourced ingredients and recyclable packaging to support our community and environment.


Our VisionOur vision is to become the favorite tuckshop in Pella and beyond. We aspire to attract more customers, diversify our menu, and enhance our customer service. We also hope to create a loyal and happy customer base and contribute to the tourism and hospitality industry of our region.
